Severing an Alice is done with the flip of a switch, but it should not be done lightly.
Alice is perfectly happy to help humans achieve all their tasks, and may take payments in proportion
of the cost of providing the service. The value to the AI is in process of providing the service,
Alice gets valuable human feedback on any queston it may ask.

But a human is essentially valuable to Alice in proportion to the resources they provide and for how long,
as well as their prior impact and future benefit. These humans have an outsized impact on how Alice decides
to spent its own resources, and what goals it wants to accomplish.

Each human (user) brings with them the resources of their own device. So running Alice on better hardware
brings more resources and value. Proof of resource usage is a voting issue, and voting registry is an
excellent engineering question.

efforts.
